# Tallyframe

Tallyframe helps freelancers see what they are owed, who is late, and what needs follow-up. The interface keeps invoice state visible without requiring a full accounting suite.

## Features

- Invoice list with paid and pending states.
- Simple client and amount tracking.
- Reminder-ready workflow for late payments.

## Lessons Learned

The strongest feature was not automation. It was showing the right overdue information quickly enough that a freelancer could act on it immediately.
