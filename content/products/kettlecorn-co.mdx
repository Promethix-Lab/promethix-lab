# Kettlecorn Co.

Kettlecorn Co. needed a polished storefront before a weekend market. The site focuses on clear product selection, fast checkout paths, and a brand system that feels local without becoming decorative.

## Features

- Responsive storefront for a small product catalogue.
- Conversion-focused product cards and checkout callouts.
- Lightweight page structure that loads quickly on mobile.

## Lessons Learned

Small consumer brands benefit from directness. Clear pricing, product photography slots, and one strong call to action mattered more than a large marketing page.
